## ⭐ THE PROMPT (the thing the whole post delivers)

> Rewrite the text below. Strip every filler phrase, hedge, and AI-ism
> ("delve", "tapestry", "in today's fast-paced world", "it's important to
> note", "moreover", "in conclusion"). Cut adverbs. Use short, concrete
> sentences. Vary sentence length so it sounds human. Keep my meaning and facts
> exactly — change nothing factual. Read it out loud in your head; if a line
> sounds like a robot wrote it, rewrite that line. Return only the edited text.
>
> TEXT: [paste your draft]

Short label for the slide / caption: **the "Sound Human" rewrite prompt.**
