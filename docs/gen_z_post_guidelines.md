# Gen Z Automation - Instagram Post Guidelines

## Overview
This document serves as the standard template and set of rules for creating high-impact Instagram posts for the Gen Z business and technology brand. Whenever requested to create a post, reference these guidelines.

## Specifications
- **Canvas Size:** 1080x1080 pixels (Square)
- **Layout:**
  - **Top 60% (648px):** Cinematic realistic image
  - **Bottom 40% (432px):** Black background text area

## Image Requirements
- **Style:** Ultra realistic, dramatic lighting, high contrast
- **Theme:** Technology or economic theme
- **Aesthetic:** Modern, cinematic, powerful composition
- **Constraints:** NO text on the image

## Typography & Text Area
- **Background:** Solid Black (`#000000`)
- **Main Headline:**
  - Bold uppercase
  - Large condensed font (e.g., Bebas Neue / Anton style)
  - Color: White with neon green highlights (`#39FF14`)
- **Hook Structure:**
  - Use a strong psychological hook based on pain, urgency, or opportunity.
  - Include a large number or strong statement.
- **Headline Format:**
  - **Line 1:** Setup (White)
  - **Line 2:** Key number or power word (Neon Green `#39FF14`)
  - **Line 3:** Consequence or outcome (White)

## Subheadline
- **Length:** 1–2 lines
- **Content:** Short explanation of why it matters
- **Color:** Gray text (e.g., `#A0A0A0`)
- **Font:** Clean, readable sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto)

## Tone & Voice
- Direct, serious, and high-stakes
- NO hype, NO emojis
- **Goal:** Make the viewer feel urgency, curiosity, or fear of missing out (FOMO).
