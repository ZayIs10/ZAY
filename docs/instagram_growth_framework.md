## STEP 0 — Instagram Algorithm Mechanics (2026)

Confirmed by Mosseri + cross-referenced sources. **Read this BEFORE any content decision.**

### 0.1 Ranking Signals — by weight (most → least)

For **Reels** (the surface that matters most for new-follower growth):
1. **Sends per reach (DM shares)** — single most heavily weighted signal in 2026
2. **Watch time / completion rate** — average % of Reel watched, replays count double
3. **Saves** — high-value, "I'll come back to this" content
4. **Shares** (public reshares to story/feed)
5. **Comments** (especially comments with >3 words)
6. **Likes** (lowest weight of the engagement actions)
7. **Profile photo taps** (signals the post made someone curious about who you are)

For **Feed** posts (carousels + photos):
1. Time spent on post (dwell)
2. Saves
3. Shares + sends
4. Comments
5. Likes
6. Profile taps

For **Explore**: speed of engagement matters more than total — early velocity wins.

### 0.2 The "Sends Per Reach" Formula
- Instagram now publicly shows creators a "sends per reach" metric in Insights
- Target: **>1% sends per reach = high distribution signal**
- Practical translation: every post must give the viewer a reason to send it to a friend
  - Relatable / "this is so me" content
  - Useful info worth bookmarking AND sharing
  - Take/opinion someone wants to debate
  - Surprising stat or visual

### 0.3 Watch Time Rules (Reels)
- First 3 seconds = pass/fail. Loses viewer here = dead reel.
- Average completion rate matters more than total views. A reel watched fully by 1,000 outperforms one stopped at 3s by 10,000.
- Replay loops count toward watch time — short, loopable reels get amplified
- Sweet spot: **7–15 seconds for top-of-funnel discovery**, up to 90s for educational
- Reels up to 3 minutes now eligible for Explore IF retention holds

### 0.4 Hooks — the first 3 seconds
- Visual hook in frame 1 (text overlay + striking image — ~50% of viewers watch muted)
- Verbal hook in first 1.5s ("Most people don't know…", "Here's why X is broken…")
- No logos, no slow intros, no "hey guys" — they kill retention before second 2

### 0.5 Originality + Aggregator Penalty
- **Watermarks from other platforms (TikTok, CapCut watermark) → automatic distribution suppression**
- Recycled / reposted content without added value → "Aggregator Penalty"
- Original assets, original audio (or trending audio used <2 weeks old) wins
- AI-generated visuals are fine; AI captions/voiceovers are fine — the penalty is for *unmodified reposts*

### 0.6 Posting Cadence
- **1 post/day = baseline** (consistency > volume)
- **Do NOT post 2 reels back-to-back within a few hours** — Instagram suppresses the second one to avoid spamming followers
- Space posts ≥ 6 hours apart, ideally one prime-time slot per day
- Stories: 2–5 per day boosts close-friend signal but doesn't directly drive feed reach

### 0.7 Hashtags + Keywords (the 2026 shift)
- Hashtag follow was removed (Dec 2024) — hashtags are now mostly a search/topic signal
- **Keywords in caption + alt text now outrank hashtags** for discovery
- Use **3–5 relevant hashtags max** (more = spam-flag risk)
- First sentence of caption = SEO. Put the topic keyword there.

### 0.8 Account Eligibility for Recommendation
Reels/posts get pulled OUT of Explore + Reels feed if:
- Watermark from another platform
- Low resolution / no audio / borders
- Engagement bait ("comment YES if…", "follow for more")
- Repeated political/borderline content
- Community-guideline strikes in last 30 days

### 0.9 Small-Account Survival Rules (under 10K followers)
- Algorithm IS friendly to small accounts now — quality signals override follower count
- Niche down hard: every post should clearly belong to ONE topic
- Reply to every comment in the first hour — boosts the post's "interaction velocity" score
- DM new followers within 24h — builds the "close connection" graph that powers Stories + Feed reach
- Don't follow/unfollow — flagged behavior, suppresses account-wide

### 0.10 The 3-Metric Scorecard (use this on every post)
After 48 hours, every post is judged by:
| Metric | Floor (delete-it line) | Good | Viral signal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sends per reach | <0.5% | 1% | >2% |
| Avg watch time | <40% | 60% | >85% |
| Saves per reach | <0.3% | 0.8% | >1.5% |

If a post hits 2 of 3 viral signals → make 5 more like it that week.
If a post hits 0 of 3 floors → archive it (do not delete — archiving avoids the "deleter" flag).

## STEP 1 — Profile Setup

### 1.1 Pick the Niche
- Rule: pick something you can post about for 2+ years without getting bored
- Know your niche, your audience, your content style
- Avoid generic "achievement" content — use inspirational/educational angle
- Mentioned sub-niches: AI/Technology, Business, Philosophy
- Tool: use Claude + Apify to scrape pages in your niche to validate

### 1.2 Username
- Memorable, easy to pronounce, correct spelling
- Avoid numbers + underscores
- Tool: Claude prompt — "Give me 30 IG handle ideas for a [niche] page. 1-2 words. No numbers."

### 1.3 Branding (Logo + Bio + Name)
- Logo: memorable, minimalistic, on-brand DNA, professional
- 3 logo styles: Icon / Full-or-partial username / Initials
- Bio format:
  - Line 1: who you are / what you do
  - Line 2: contact info
  - Line 3: CTA + link in bio
- Tool: feed Claude 10 top competitor bios → ask for 20 variations

### 1.4 Time to Start Posting
- Have content ready for first 14 posts before launching

## STEP 2 — Viral Content Engine (the AI upgrade)

### 2.1 Find What's Working
- Pick 10 competitors in your niche (50K–2M followers)
- Apify scrapes top 30 posts from each → 1 spreadsheet ranked by saves
- Optional: use Visualfruit for stat-based competitor research
- Rule: don't invent. Originality only matters AFTER 1000 followers

### 2.2 Create a Content Pipeline
- Pick 1–2 days/week to batch ALL content (e.g. Sunday morning)
- Plan a clear weekly overview

### 2.3 Content Creation
- Tools shown: Imagen / Google DeepMind / Pictory.io for images, video scenes, voiceovers
- Sources: YouTube + Pinterest for raw footage / images, CapCut for Reel edits

### 2.4 Post Daily + Track What Wins
- Minimum 1 post/day
- Every 7 days: look at top 3 posts → find the pattern
- If a post hits average → make more like it immediately
- Apify scrapes YOUR pages nightly → Claude flags what's working in a weekly report

## STEP 4 — Scaling (AI = operator)

### 4.1 Start with 1 Page First
- Don't build 5 pages at once → you'll burn out on all 5
- Get ONE page to 100K + revenue BEFORE scaling
- This is the proof. No AI shortcut allowed.

### 4.2 Build Repeatable SOPs
- Feed Claude your top 50 winning posts from Page 1 → get a repeatable SOP
- Any editor can then run the next page using the playbook
- Document everything from day 1

### 4.3 Daily Updates (Scrapes)
- Apify + Claude watch everything nightly
- Scrapes all your competitor pages → Google Sheets
- Every morning Claude briefs you on what grew, what hit, what's dying

### 4.4 Automate Posting (n8n)
1. Build Notion board with "Ready to Post" status — every card has media, caption, post time, target page
2. n8n watches Notion → when card hits "Ready to Post", calls Meta Graph API to publish
3. After posting → n8n updates card to "Posted" → Apify pulls performance data back into Notion 48 hours later

### 4.5 Repeat The Entire Cycle

- Monetization (Step 3) is locked behind follower count — skip until after 10K

- Reels are now the highest-leverage missing feature, ahead of competitor scraping
- Notion vs Google Sheets is a substitution, not a missing feature — current Sheets-based flow works
- Watermark rule means: never repost from TikTok / never include CapCut export watermark — we already comply
