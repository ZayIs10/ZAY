Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gen Z Capital ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our Instagram automation application.

1. What This App Does

This application is an internal automation tool used solely by Gen Z Capital to publish educational financial content to our own Instagram account. It does not collect, store, or share data from any third-party users.

2. Data We Access

  • Our own Instagram Business account profile and media (via instagram_basic)
  • The ability to publish posts to our own Instagram account (via instagram_content_publish)

We do not access, collect, or store data from any other Instagram users or accounts.

3. How We Use Data

Data accessed through the Instagram API is used exclusively to automate the posting of content to the Gen Z Capital Instagram account. No data is sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage

We do not store any user data from Instagram. Access tokens are stored securely as environment variables and are never logged or shared.

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses the Facebook/Instagram Graph API. Use of these services is governed by Meta's Privacy Policy.
